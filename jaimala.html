Jaimala theme

For a Hindu wedding ceremony, the varmala or jaimala (wedding garland consisting of fresh flowers) is essential. It is one of the marriage's first rites. One of the most treasured moments is the marriage ceremony. We make sure that we provide you with the best quality of garlands so that your moment becomes a memorable one.

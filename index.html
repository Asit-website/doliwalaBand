As a company with licensed expert band artists, we take pride in serving our clients throughout the length and breadth of Delhi. At Doliband, we understand how band music and melody can translate your dream wedding night into reality. Therefore, we employ well-trained experts at your service and also equip them with the latest musical instruments to ensure their best performance for your wedding night. Whether you are fond of dhol or trumpet or any other musical instrument we have band artists who are proficient in playing all these instruments.

Our Services

Band Baaja

Band Baja sets up the tone for a wedding night. We are well aware of this. Therefore, at Doliwala band, we have a team comprising of the best artists to add new life to your wedding celebration. All our artists specialize in offering the wedding band services of the highest quality for wedding baraat. They play the most recent musical numbers to complement your baraat.

Ghori & Baggi

At Doliband, we have the finest-bred mares as ghodi for your wedding night. They are snowy white, intelligent, disciplined, and strong. We value the presence of our ghodis on your wedding night. Therefore, we feed and do everything possible to care for them so you can build a solid impression with them at the time of your entrance.

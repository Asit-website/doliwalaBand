Punjabi Dhol

You get tingles when you hear an Indian drum in the distance because it signals the approach of something loud, proud, and exciting. A colorful parade carrying a bridegroom to

his wedding, where he will be united with his wife, is typically followed by the approaching noise. Due to this, the dhol is a crucial component of any South Asian wedding, and we now offer direct access to the best dhol players in town!

With many years of experience performing at events, our Dhol Players are drummers with professional training, so we can assure you that you won't be dissatisfied. From bride and groom entrances at weddings to performance sets for you to dance to, we will accommodate your needs.
